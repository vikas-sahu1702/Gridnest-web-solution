About Gridnest

We build digital experiences that transform businesses and elevate brands across the globe.

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Gridnest Web Solutions began with a singular vision: to craft digital experiences that don't just look stunning — they deliver measurable business results. What started as a small team of passionate designers and developers in Lucknow has grown into an international agency trusted by businesses across 40+ countries.

We recognized early on that the hospitality industry and corporate sector deserved far better digital solutions than what the market offered. Generic templates and one-size-fits-all approaches weren't cutting it. Hotels needed booking-integrated websites that converted. Restaurants needed online ordering systems that actually worked. Corporates needed platforms that reflected their stature and drove growth.

That gap became our mission. Over the past decade, we've delivered 150+ premium websites, helped clients increase their online bookings by an average of 45%, and built lasting partnerships based on trust, transparency, and exceptional craftsmanship.
